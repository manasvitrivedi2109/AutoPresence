How Leave Management Works in AutoPresence

AutoPresence provides an AI-driven leave management system that allows college students to request and track their leave digitally. The system integrates face and voice recognition for secure authentication.

Steps to Apply for Leave

1. Log in to AutoPresence using face and voice recognition.
2. Navigate to the 'Leave Management' section.
3. Fill in the leave request form with the necessary details.
4. Submit your request for approval.
5. Track the status of your leave request in real-time.

Benefits

- Secure authentication using AI.
- Easy tracking of leave requests.
- Quick approval process.
- Digital records for transparency.
