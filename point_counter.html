How the Points System Works

AutoPresence assigns points based on attendance. Each successful attendance check-in grants points, while absences may deduct points. The more consistent you are, the higher your score!

Status Levels

Students are assigned different status levels based on their attendance points:

  • Excellent - 90%+ attendance
  • Good - 75-89% attendance
  • Needs Improvement - Below 75%

Benefits of High Attendance

Maintaining a high attendance rate can earn you rewards, certificates, and even special privileges within the college!
