How AutoPresence Works

AutoPresence is an advanced AI-driven face and voice recognition system designed for college students' attendance tracking. It streamlines the traditional attendance process by utilizing cutting-edge biometric authentication.

1. Face and Voice Recognition: Students authenticate their identity through a combination of facial and voice recognition to ensure secure and accurate attendance marking.

2. AI-Powered Verification: The system leverages AI to compare the captured face and voice patterns with stored data, minimizing the risk of proxy attendance.

3. Real-Time Attendance Update: Once verified, attendance is marked in real time, and students receive instant confirmation.

4. Secure Data Handling: All authentication data is securely stored and encrypted to maintain privacy and integrity.

5. Gamification & Rewards: Students earn points for consistent attendance, motivating punctuality and participation.
